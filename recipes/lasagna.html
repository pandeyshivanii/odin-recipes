Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that consists of layers of pasta, a savory meat sauce, béchamel (white sauce), and cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal perfect for family gatherings or dinner parties.

The key to making a delicious lasagna is in the layers. Start by layering cooked lasagna noodles, followed by a rich tomato meat sauce, then béchamel sauce, and finally a generous amount of mozzarella and parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers and bake in the oven until golden and bubbly.

Lasagna can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for a day or two, making it a great meal for busy weeknights or entertaining guests.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups béchamel sauce
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Brown the ground beef in a pan, then add marinara sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer the noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, béchamel, and cheeses. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  5. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes or until golden and bubbly.
  6. Let it cool for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
