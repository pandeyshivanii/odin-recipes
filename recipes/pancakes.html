Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a classic breakfast favorite, known for their fluffy texture and versatility. They are made with a simple batter that cooks quickly, creating soft, golden-brown pancakes. Perfectly paired with syrup, butter, or fruits, pancakes are the ideal start to your day.

The beauty of pancakes lies in their simplicity. Whether you like them plain or topped with whipped cream, berries, or chocolate chips, pancakes offer endless possibilities for customization. They're easy to make and can be prepared in just 20 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Butter or oil for frying
  • Optional toppings: syrup, berries, whipped cream, or chocolate chips

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmixâ€”the batter should be slightly lumpy.
  4. Heat a griddle or frying pan over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  5. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with your favorite toppings such as syrup, butter, fruit, or whipped cream.
